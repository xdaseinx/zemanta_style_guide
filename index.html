Brand

The brand elements include logo, sub brands, colors and typeface.
To maintain consistency, proportions must not be altered.

Logo

Logo color variations

COLOR VERSION

Spot logo, one color logo, for use on white backgrounds or where there is sufficient contrast between the logo and the background for reproduction.

BLACK VERSION

Black logo - for use when color reproduction is not an option

WHITE VERSION

Where the background does not contract sufficiently strongly with the full color Master Brand Identity, the white version should be used.

Clear and white space around the identity

The Zemanta logo should have the following minimum amounts of space around it. The height of the " Z " forms the clear or white space.
