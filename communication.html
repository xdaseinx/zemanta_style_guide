Communication

When we communicate outwards our choice of words is what makes our message clear and consistent. Throughout Zemanta, the language we use must communicate our value of trust, as we nurture it internally as we do externally.

Readers

A Reader is any person who uses one of our Publisher's sites. Having interacted with the site, they should adopt the use of our widget as a primary navigation tool.

The emotions they should feel are:

  • trust that the link will lead them to good content
  • adventure having no prior knowledge of the destination site
  • a feeling of completeness having finished interacting with content on the source site

Publishers

A Publisher is a person or an organization that provides inventory for our recommendations. The main touch points are Zemanta Reports and email.

The emotions they should feel are:

  • complete confidence in all provided data
  • safety—an overview of their inventory, earnings
  • courage, because they are in control
  • pride over how well the campaigns are doing

Brands

A Brand is a person or an organization that buys traffic from our network. The main touch points are Zemanta Reports and email communication.

The emotions they should feel are:

  • complete confidence in all provided data
  • safety—an overview of traffic, costs and results
  • control over where they spend their budget
  • appreciation of how Zemanta helps build their brand
